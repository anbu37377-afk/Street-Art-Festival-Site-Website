Spray Art Fundamentals Workshop

Master the art of spray painting with professional guidance. Perfect for beginners and intermediate artists looking to enhance their skills.

Book Now View Pricing

About This Workshop

Join our comprehensive spray art fundamentals workshop and learn from experienced street artists in a supportive, creative environment.

What You'll Learn

Safety First

Proper ventilation, mask usage, and safe handling of spray paint equipment.

Equipment Basics

Understanding different spray cans, nozzles, and their effects on your artwork.

Basic Techniques

Master fundamental spray techniques including lines, fills, and fades.

Color Theory

Understanding color combinations and how they work in street art.

Stencil Creation

Design and cut your own stencils for repeatable patterns and designs.

Composition

Learn how to create balanced and impactful compositions on walls or canvas.
