Mission & Vision

What drives us to celebrate street art

Our Mission

To democratize art by bringing it out of galleries and into the streets, making creativity accessible to everyone regardless of background or socioeconomic status.

  • Empower emerging artists
  • Transform public spaces
  • Foster community engagement
  • Promote cultural diversity

Our Vision

To create a world where every city street is a gallery, every wall tells a story, and art is an integral part of daily urban life.

  • Global street art network
  • Sustainable art practices
  • Educational programs
  • Cultural preservation

Our Journey

From a small gathering to a global movement

2020 - The Beginning

Started as a small community event with 15 local artists and 500 visitors in a single neighborhood.

2021 - Growth

Expanded to 3 locations, featuring 50 artists and attracting 3,000 visitors from across the region.

2022 - Recognition

Received national arts funding and featured in major media outlets. Grew to 100 artists and 8,000 visitors.

2023 - International

Went international with artists from 15 countries. Launched educational workshops and youth programs.

2024 - Innovation

Introduced digital art installations, VR experiences, and sustainable art practices. Reached 15,000 visitors.

2026 - The Future

Planning our biggest festival yet with 200+ artists, multiple venues, and global live streaming.
